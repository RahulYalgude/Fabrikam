About Company

Multinational corporation (MNC), also called transnational corporation, any corporation that is registered and operates in more than one country at a time. Fabrikam is a multinational company has their 24 Offices all over world. Company wanted to build and host Information Management Solutions in web. Company also wants to host internet public web site for their potential customers to know about products and services provided. They required to build and host intranet application for all employees to access Human Resource, Accounting, Project Management related information. Fabrikam has public Cloud subscription to use. Generally the corporation has its headquarters in one country and operates wholly or partially owned subsidiaries in other countries.
